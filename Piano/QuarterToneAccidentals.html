Quarter-tone Accidentals

These are examples of the most common notation for music involving quarter tones. (Microtonal notation in Western music is not widely standardized and you may see other symbols used instead of the ones below.)

Demiflat
Lowers the pitch of a note by one quarter tone.
Flat-and-a-half
Lowers the pitch of a note by three quarter tones.
Demisharp
Raises the pitch of a note by one quarter tone.
Sharp-and-a-half
Raises the pitch of a note by three quarter tones.
